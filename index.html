How Does Gamification Work?

Lintellect generates a great learning experience, making it familiar to something that you already know, a game.

How learning becomes fun?

Socializing what you learn with friends, peers and mentors, challenging your skills and getting rewards for leveling up.

What is our Coin?

Is a virtual currency used exclusively on Lintellect, any user can buy them, but only mentors can monetize coins.

How can I become a mentor?

We provide easy to use tools for anyone with a valuable skill to be able to teach. You can monetize your content or share it for free.

How does Lintellect gives back to communities?

We aim to collect knowledge from our users and mentors and descentralize it, making it available for anyone. Content shared for free will be free to access for everyone on Lintellect.

Why are we on BETA?

Is not easy to develop a concept like Lintellect. So, we want your help to shape the future of learning.
